Digital Signage System

Digital signage system in which video content, advertisements, and/or messages are displayed on digital signs with the goal of delivering targeted messages, to specific locations and/or consumers, at specific times.Digital signage system used in public spaces, transportation systems, museums, stadiums, retail stores, hotels, restaurants, and corporate buildings etc.

Digital signage system is visually dynamic, engaging and flexible. Digital signage system can be used for a wide range of applications such as education, healthcare, restaurant, retail, showroom, lobby reception, cafeterias, break rooms, call centers, elevator banks, manufacturing floors, railway stations... It is a natural choice for reaching large audience.

  • It is not only informs and engages more people through creative presentation, but also reaches them instantly.
  • Messages can be updated or retired in real-time.
  • It is a modern approach to communications that draws in viewers with bright, animated graphics and interactivity.
  • It allows you to create, import and manage your messages and alerts.
  • Messages can be delivered to your audience within a minute.

Features

  • Display supported HDMI 1080p(Full HD) and Analog output.
  • It supports major image formats such as JPG, GIF, PNG, BMP.
  • Video formats supported are AVI, MP4, MKV.
  • Audio formats supported are WAV, WMA, MP3.


Digital Signage System Advantages

Various advantages of Digital signage system. Uses of Digital signage system across different premises are shown below.

Education

Digital signage system is used to show various facilities of the educational institutions and display the schedules of events conducted, annoucements, messages etc.

Healthcare

Digital signage system in hospital can serve many purposes, from helping visitors find their way around a large hospital to informing and entertaining patients as they sit in the waiting room.

Restaurant

Digital signage system is used in restaurants to instantaneously update menus, enhance the interior design, up-sell featured products and enhance sales.

Retail

Digital signage system can improve the shopping experience, enabling customers to make more informed decisions and helping retailors to make their stores more atttractive and improve. Can easily attract and keep your customers.

Showroom

Digital signage system in showroom displays offer much more then a simple slide show by enhancing the message with branding, marketing, specials etc.
