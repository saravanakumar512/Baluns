Vision Viewer System

Vision viewer system is Linux ARM based Viewing applications System for High speed imaging.

Vision Viewer System plays a major role in high speed viewing application for inspecting the prodcution. Vision viewer system is equipped with Hihg resolution CMOS cameras to acquire the images of prodcution line at faster rate and high quality. Using this system opeartor can easily identify the defects in prodcution line. It is highly used in printing industries for inspecting the qulaity of print while printing. It improves the quality and prodcution.
